Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how AgroHelper collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly, such as registration details (email) and images you upload for plant disease analysis. Camera access is used only with your explicit permission.

2. Data Usage

Images are processed using AI models to provide diagnostic reports. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Account & Data Deletion

User Right: You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, please contact us at support@agrohelper.ge. We will process your request within 48 hours.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and Google Analytics for app performance. These services have their own privacy policies.
